About KarMi

KarMi is an Afro-Latinx artist / mobile-phone photographer based from Philadelphia, PA. They utilize traditional artistic methods on unconventional portible devices to break through and connect on a deeper level with their subject. Whether it is in their lab or on the street, KarMi is able to tell vivid stories with their timeless work.
